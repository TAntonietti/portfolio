# PALADYR AR

Economic Intelligence Platform for Argentina focused on macroeconomic
monitoring, financial markets, energy, mining and agricultural
intelligence through a unified dashboard.

## Scope

-   Argentina macroeconomic dashboard
-   BCRA & INDEC monitoring
-   Exchange rates & Country Risk
-   Financial markets
-   Energy intelligence
-   Mining intelligence
-   Agricultural markets
-   Commodity tracking
-   Automated news aggregation
-   AI research assistant
-   Interactive dashboards
-   Historical database
