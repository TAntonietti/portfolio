# VisionCare

Digital ecosystem concept designed to integrate patient services,
clinical workflows and business intelligence into a single platform for
ophthalmology clinics.

## Scope

-   Website
-   AI Assistant
-   Appointment management
-   Patient portal
-   Process automation
-   Executive dashboards
-   System integration

## Repository

-   Digital ecosystem architecture
-   Patient journey
-   AI-assisted patient support
-   Clinical workflow integration
-   Executive dashboard concept
-   Implementation roadmap

## Status

Concept proposal for an integrated digital ecosystem supporting patient
experience, operational efficiency and data-driven decision making.

Commercial and operational information has been simplified for portfolio
purposes.
