# HELIOS

Financial process automation platform developed to optimize Accounts
Payable, Tax Reporting and Business Intelligence workflows.

## Scope

-   Accounts Payable automation
-   OCR-based invoice processing
-   Tax reporting
-   Business Intelligence dashboards
-   Data validation and reconciliation
-   Executive reporting

## Results

-   AP cycle reduced from 40+ to 4 days
-   80% reduction in manual processing time
-   80% of invoice volume automated
-   99.8% OCR accuracy for key suppliers
-   USD 24,000 annual discrepancy recovery
-   Integrated AP, TAX and BI workflows
